Tour Overview

This thoughtfully crafted itinerary invites you to explore Georgia's most iconic landmarks alongside its best-kept secrets. Wander through the vibrant streets of Tbilisi, marvel at the serene vistas of the Caucasus Mountains, and uncover the stories behind historic monasteries and modern wine estates. Every day offers a unique blend of cultural richness, historical depth, and natural beauty. Indulge in traditional Georgian cuisine and experience the legendary warmth of Georgian hospitality as you delve into the heart of one of the world's most captivating countries. Designed for travelers seeking a comprehensive and unforgettable journey, this tour captures the very essence of Georgia's charm and diversity.

Tour Itinerary [+]

Day 1: Arrival and City Tour in Tbilisi

[+]

Begin your comprehensive Georgian adventure with a warm welcome and city tour of Tbilisi. Explore the vibrant capital's rich history and culture, including visits to iconic sites like Narikala Fortress, sulfur baths, and Freedom Square. Wander through the charming streets and get acquainted with Georgian hospitality on this introductory day that sets the stage for your 10-day journey through this captivating country.

  • Arrival in Tbilisi and airport transfer;
  • Comprehensive city tour of the capital;
  • Visit Narikala Fortress and Old Town;
  • Explore traditional sulfur baths area;
  • See Freedom Square and central landmarks;
  • Introduction to Georgian culture and history;
  • Check-in and overnight in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 4-5 hours

Day 2: Tbilisi – Ananuri – Kazbegi

[+]

Journey from Tbilisi through stunning mountain landscapes to reach the breathtaking region of Kazbegi. Stop at the magnificent Ananuri Fortress complex beside the Aragvi River, with its dramatic mountain backdrop. Continue through the scenic Gudauri mountain pass, taking in panoramic views from the Gudauri Friendship Monument before arriving in the mountain town of Kazbegi for spectacular Caucasus vistas.

  • Departure from Tbilisi to mountain regions;
  • Visit stunning Ananuri Fortress complex;
  • Scenic drive through Aragvi River valley;
  • Stop at Gudauri Friendship Monument;
  • Enjoy panoramic mountain views;
  • Arrival in breathtaking Kazbegi;
  • Check-in and overnight in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours

Day 3: Kazbegi

[+]

Immerse yourself fully in the spectacular mountain scenery of Kazbegi. Visit the iconic Gergeti Trinity Church, perched dramatically at 2,170 meters with unparalleled views of Mount Kazbek and the surrounding Caucasus peaks. Explore the beautiful Gveleti Waterfalls and discover the dramatic landscapes of Dariali Gorge, experiencing the raw natural beauty of this mountain paradise.

  • Full day exploring Kazbegi region;
  • Visit iconic Gergeti Trinity Church;
  • Experience breathtaking Caucasus views;
  • Explore beautiful Gveleti Waterfalls;
  • Discover dramatic Dariali Gorge;
  • Photography opportunities in stunning landscapes;
  • Overnight stay in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours

Day 4: Kazbegi – Stalin Museum in Gori – Uplistsikhe – Tbilisi

[+]

Travel from the mountains to explore Georgia's fascinating history. Visit Gori, known for the Stalin Museum, then explore the remarkable ancient rock-hewn city of Uplistsikhe, one of Georgia's most important archaeological sites. This day combines recent history with ancient heritage, showcasing the diverse cultural layers that make Georgia so captivating, before returning to Tbilisi.

  • Departure from Kazbegi mountain region;
  • Visit Stalin Museum in Gori;
  • Explore remarkable Uplistsikhe cave city;
  • Discover ancient rock-hewn architecture;
  • Learn about archaeological significance;
  • Journey back to Tbilisi;
  • Check-in and overnight in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 4 hours total

Day 5: Tbilisi - Gelati - Motsameta - Kutaisi

[+]

Journey to western Georgia to explore one of the country's oldest cities, Kutaisi. Visit the magnificent UNESCO-listed Gelati Monastery, a masterpiece of medieval Georgian architecture, and the serene Motsameta Monastery. These historic religious sites showcase Georgia's rich spiritual heritage and architectural achievements, representing centuries of Georgian Orthodox tradition in beautiful settings.

  • Travel to historic Kutaisi in western Georgia;
  • Visit UNESCO-listed Gelati Monastery;
  • Explore magnificent medieval architecture;
  • See serene Motsameta Monastery;
  • Learn about Georgian Orthodox heritage;
  • Discover spiritual and cultural significance;
  • Check-in and overnight in Kutaisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 4.5 hours

Day 6: Kutaisi – Gelati – Prometheus Cave – Martvili Canyon – Kutaisi

[+]

Experience the natural wonders of western Georgia with visits to stunning underground and surface attractions. Explore the magnificent Prometheus Cave with its spectacular stalactites and underground chambers. Then enjoy the picturesque beauty of Martvili Canyon, where you can take a boat trip along turquoise waters surrounded by dramatic canyon walls, creating unforgettable memories of Georgia's natural splendor.

  • Return visit to Gelati Monastery;
  • Explore magnificent Prometheus Cave;
  • Discover spectacular underground chambers;
  • Experience boat trip at Martvili Canyon;
  • Enjoy turquoise waters and canyon views;
  • Photography in stunning natural settings;
  • Return to Kutaisi for overnight.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours

Day 7: Kutaisi – Batumi Botanical Garden – Local Winery - Batumi

[+]

Travel to Georgia's stunning Black Sea coast and the vibrant city of Batumi. Begin with a visit to the world-renowned Batumi Botanical Garden, featuring diverse plant collections from around the globe in spectacular coastal settings. Experience a wine tour and tasting at a local winery, celebrating Georgia's famous wine heritage while enjoying the refreshing sea breeze and coastal atmosphere.

  • Journey to Black Sea coast and Batumi;
  • Visit world-renowned Batumi Botanical Garden;
  • Explore diverse international plant collections;
  • Wine tour and tasting at local winery;
  • Celebrate Georgian wine heritage;
  • Enjoy coastal atmosphere and sea views;
  • Check-in and overnight in Batumi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 2.5 hours

Day 8: Batumi City Tour, Free Evening

[+]

Discover the charm and attractions of coastal Batumi with a comprehensive city tour. Visit Europe Square, see the iconic Ali and Nino Statue, and explore the city's unique blend of modern architecture and traditional Georgian elements. Enjoy a free evening to relax on the beach, stroll along the seaside boulevard, or experience Batumi's vibrant nightlife and dining scene at your own pace.

  • Comprehensive Batumi city tour;
  • Visit elegant Europe Square;
  • See iconic Ali and Nino moving statue;
  • Explore modern and traditional architecture;
  • Free evening for personal exploration;
  • Optional beach relaxation and seaside strolls;
  • Overnight stay in Batumi.
  • Duration of the tour: 4-5 hours, then free time

Day 9: Batumi – Tbilisi (Train)

[+]

Experience a scenic and comfortable train journey from the Black Sea coast back to Georgia's capital. The train ride offers beautiful views of Georgian landscapes as you travel from the coastal region through diverse terrain back to Tbilisi. This relaxing journey provides time to reflect on your Georgian adventures while enjoying the changing scenery from the comfort of the train.

  • Scenic train journey from Batumi to Tbilisi;
  • Comfortable rail travel experience;
  • Beautiful views of Georgian landscapes;
  • Relaxing journey through diverse terrain;
  • Time to reflect on your adventures;
  • Arrival back in Tbilisi;
  • Check-in and final overnight in Tbilisi.
  • Duration: Full day train journey

Day 10: Departure from Tbilisi

[+]

Conclude your comprehensive 10-day journey through Georgia with final moments in Tbilisi. Enjoy a leisurely breakfast and take time for any last-minute shopping or exploration. Transfer to Tbilisi International Airport for your departure, carrying with you unforgettable memories of Georgia's diverse landscapes, rich culture, ancient heritage, delicious cuisine, and the warmth of Georgian hospitality from this complete exploration of the country.

  • Final leisurely breakfast in Tbilisi;
  • Last-minute shopping or exploration;
  • Hotel check-out and airport transfer;
  • Departure from Tbilisi International Airport;
  • End of comprehensive 10-day Georgian adventure;
  • Memories of diverse landscapes and rich culture.
  • Duration: As per flight schedule
