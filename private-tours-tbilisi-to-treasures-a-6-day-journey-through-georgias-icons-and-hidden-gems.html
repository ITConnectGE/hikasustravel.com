Tour Overview

Georgia's rich culture, history, and natural beauty await on this 6-day tour that begins and ends in the vibrant city of Tbilisi. Stroll through the cobblestone streets of Tbilisi's Old Town, uncover the secrets of the ancient rock-hewn city of Uplistsikhe, and admire the timeless elegance of Bagrati Cathedral and Gelati Monastery—both UNESCO World Heritage Sites. As you journey through Georgia's lush landscapes, relax in the healing waters of Borjomi's mineral springs and be amazed by the serene beauty of Martvili Canyon. Along the way, indulge in traditional Georgian dishes paired with exquisite local wines, creating a culinary journey as delightful as the sights. This tour offers a perfect blend of history, culture, and nature, promising a truly immersive experience.

Tour Itinerary [+]

Day 1: Arrival and city tour in Tbilisi

[+]

Begin your Georgian treasure hunt with arrival in Tbilisi, the vibrant capital where ancient history meets modern life. Explore the charming cobblestone streets of the Old Town, visit the iconic Narikala Fortress with panoramic city views, cross the modern Peace Bridge, and discover the magnificent Holy Trinity Cathedral. End your first day with a welcome dinner featuring traditional Georgian cuisine and local wines.

  • Arrival in Tbilisi and hotel check-in;
  • Explore Tbilisi Old Town;
  • Visit Narikala Fortress;
  • Walk across Peace Bridge;
  • See Holy Trinity Cathedral;
  • Welcome dinner in Tbilisi;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 4-5 hours
  • Driving duration: 30 minutes

Day 2: Tbilisi – Uplistsikhe – Gori – Borjomi

[+]

Journey into Georgia's ancient past today with a visit to Uplistsikhe, the fascinating rock-hewn town that once served as a major trading center in the early Iron Age. Explore its remarkable cave dwellings and tunnels carved into the rocky cliffs. Continue to Gori to visit the Stalin Museum, then travel to Borjomi, famous for its natural mineral waters and beautiful Central Park surrounded by pristine nature.

  • Visit Uplistsikhe ancient rock-hewn town;
  • Explore cave dwellings and tunnels;
  • Visit Stalin Museum in Gori;
  • Travel to Borjomi;
  • Walk in Borjomi Central Park;
  • Taste famous Borjomi mineral water;
  • Overnight stay in Borjomi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 3.5 hours

Day 3: Borjomi – Vardzia – Akhaltsikhe

[+]

Today discover one of Georgia's most spectacular archaeological sites. Visit Vardzia, the stunning cave monastery complex carved into the cliffs of the Erusheti Mountain during the reign of Queen Tamar in the 12th century. This remarkable site stands as a monument to Georgia's medieval history and religious life. Continue to Akhaltsikhe to explore the historic Rabati Castle, a symbol of the region's diverse cultural heritage.

  • Visit Vardzia cave monastery complex;
  • Explore medieval cave dwellings and churches;
  • Learn about Queen Tamar's legacy;
  • Travel to Akhaltsikhe;
  • Explore Rabati Castle fortress;
  • Discover diverse cultural heritage;
  • Overnight stay in Akhaltsikhe.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours

Day 4: Akhaltsikhe – Kutaisi

[+]

Journey to Kutaisi, Georgia's ancient western city with over 3,000 years of history. Upon arrival, explore the UNESCO World Heritage Sites that make this city special: visit the magnificent Bagrati Cathedral with its commanding hilltop position and the stunning Gelati Monastery, representing the golden age of medieval Georgian culture and architecture. Experience the blend of ancient heritage and modern Georgian life.

  • Scenic drive to Kutaisi;
  • Visit Bagrati Cathedral (UNESCO World Heritage Site);
  • Explore Gelati Monastery complex;
  • Discover medieval Georgian architecture;
  • Walk through Kutaisi city center;
  • Overnight stay in Kutaisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 3.5 hours

Day 5: Kutaisi – Martvili Canyon – Tbilisi

[+]

Discover one of Georgia's hidden gems today with a visit to the breathtaking Martvili Canyon. Take a boat trip through its emerald-green waters and explore the narrow gorge surrounded by lush vegetation. This natural wonder offers stunning scenery and a peaceful escape into nature. Afterward, return to Tbilisi for your final evening, reflecting on the incredible journey through Georgia's icons and hidden treasures.

  • Visit Martvili Canyon (hidden gem);
  • Take boat trip through emerald waters;
  • Explore narrow gorge with lush vegetation;
  • Enjoy stunning natural scenery;
  • Scenic drive back to Tbilisi;
  • Free evening in Tbilisi;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 4 hours

Day 6: Departure from Tbilisi

[+]

Enjoy your final morning in Tbilisi with free time to explore any remaining sights, pick up souvenirs, or simply relax and savor your last Georgian breakfast. Check out from your hotel and transfer to Tbilisi International Airport for your departure, carrying with you unforgettable memories of Georgia's most iconic landmarks and hidden treasures.

  • Final morning in Tbilisi;
  • Free time for last-minute exploration;
  • Hotel check-out;
  • Transfer to Tbilisi International Airport;
  • Departure from Georgia.
  • Duration: As per flight schedule
