Tour Overview

Unearth the rich layers of Georgia's history, culture, and natural beauty on this 9-day adventure. From the dynamic streets of Tbilisi to the serene shores of the Black Sea in Batumi, this tour offers an in-depth exploration of the country's most iconic landmarks. Walk the spiritual pathways of Mtskheta, Georgia's ancient capital, and explore the rock-hewn marvel of Uplistsikhe. In Kutaisi, admire the UNESCO-listed Gelati Monastery and the enchanting Prometheus Caves. Discover the dramatic cave city of Vardzia and stand in awe of the imposing Rabati Fortress. The journey concludes in the vibrant city of Batumi, where you can unwind amidst lush botanical gardens and stunning seaside vistas. Every day promises immersive experiences, from expertly guided tours to encounters with Georgia's legendary hospitality and cuisine.

Tour Itinerary [+]

Day 1: Arrival in Tbilisi

[+]

Begin your 9-day Georgian adventure with arrival in Tbilisi, the vibrant capital that beautifully blends ancient history with modern life. Airport transfer to your hotel for check-in and relaxation. Explore the nearby areas if time permits, getting your first taste of Georgian hospitality and culture. Prepare for the incredible journey ahead through Georgia's most iconic landmarks and hidden treasures.

  • Arrival in Tbilisi;
  • Airport pick-up and transfer to hotel;
  • Hotel check-in and relaxation;
  • Optional evening stroll in nearby areas;
  • Welcome briefing about the tour;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration: As per arrival schedule

Day 2: Tbilisi City Tour

[+]

Discover the dynamic streets and rich history of Tbilisi on a comprehensive city tour. Explore the charming Old Town with its narrow cobblestone streets, visit the ancient Narikala Fortress overlooking the city, and admire the magnificent Holy Trinity Cathedral. Walk through the historic districts and experience the vibrant blend of traditional Georgian architecture with modern landmarks that defines this captivating capital city.

  • Explore Tbilisi Old Town;
  • Visit ancient Narikala Fortress;
  • See Holy Trinity Cathedral;
  • Walk through historic districts;
  • Discover traditional and modern architecture;
  • Learn about Tbilisi's rich history;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 4-5 hours
  • Driving duration: 30 minutes

Day 3: Tbilisi – Mtskheta – Uplistsikhe – Gori – Kutaisi

[+]

Journey through Georgia's spiritual and archaeological heritage today. Walk the spiritual pathways of Mtskheta, Georgia's ancient capital and UNESCO World Heritage Site, visiting Jvari Monastery and Svetitskhoveli Cathedral. Explore the remarkable rock-hewn marvel of Uplistsikhe, one of Georgia's most significant archaeological sites. Visit Gori with its famous Stalin Museum before arriving in Kutaisi, one of Georgia's oldest cities with over 3,000 years of history.

  • Visit Mtskheta (UNESCO World Heritage Site);
  • Explore Jvari Monastery and Svetitskhoveli Cathedral;
  • Discover Uplistsikhe ancient rock-hewn town;
  • Visit Stalin Museum in Gori;
  • Arrive in historic Kutaisi;
  • Check-in and overnight in Kutaisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 4.5 hours

Day 4: Kutaisi – Gelati Monastery – Prometheus Cave – Kutaisi

[+]

Explore the cultural and natural treasures around Kutaisi today. Admire the UNESCO-listed Gelati Monastery, representing the golden age of medieval Georgian culture and architecture founded in 1106. Continue to the enchanting Prometheus Cave, a stunning natural limestone cave system with spectacular stalactites, stalagmites, and underground rivers. This perfect blend of spiritual heritage and natural wonder showcases the diverse beauty of Georgia.

  • Visit UNESCO-listed Gelati Monastery;
  • Explore medieval Georgian architecture;
  • Tour enchanting Prometheus Cave;
  • Marvel at stalactites and stalagmites;
  • Experience underground rivers;
  • Return to Kutaisi;
  • Overnight stay in Kutaisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 1.5 hours

Day 5: Kutaisi – Vardzia Cave – Akhaltsikhe

[+]

Discover one of Georgia's most spectacular archaeological and religious sites today. Visit the dramatic cave city of Vardzia, a fascinating cave monastery complex carved into the cliffs of the Erusheti Mountain during the 12th century reign of Queen Tamar. This remarkable site showcases the ingenuity of medieval Georgian engineering and the deep religious devotion of its builders. Continue to Akhaltsikhe, a historic town with diverse cultural influences.

  • Visit spectacular Vardzia cave monastery;
  • Explore 12th-century cave dwellings;
  • Learn about Queen Tamar's legacy;
  • Marvel at medieval Georgian engineering;
  • Travel to historic Akhaltsikhe;
  • Check-in and overnight in Akhaltsikhe.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 4 hours

Day 6: Akhaltsikhe – Rabati Fortress – Borjomi – Batumi

[+]

Stand in awe of the imposing Rabati Fortress in Akhaltsikhe, a magnificent restored medieval complex showcasing the diverse architectural influences of Georgian, Ottoman, and European cultures. Continue to Borjomi, famous for its natural mineral springs and healing waters. End your day's journey in the vibrant coastal city of Batumi on the Black Sea, where modern architecture meets traditional Georgian hospitality.

  • Explore imposing Rabati Fortress;
  • Discover diverse architectural influences;
  • Visit Borjomi mineral springs;
  • Taste famous Borjomi mineral water;
  • Scenic drive to Black Sea coast;
  • Arrive in vibrant Batumi;
  • Check-in and overnight in Batumi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 4.5 hours

Day 7: Batumi City Tour

[+]

Unwind amidst the lush beauty and coastal charm of Batumi today. Explore the famous Batumi Botanical Garden with its diverse plant collections from around the world, walk through the elegant Europe Square, discover the romantic Piazza Square, and witness the moving Ali and Nino statue. Enjoy the stunning seaside vistas along Batumi Boulevard and experience the perfect blend of natural beauty and modern city attractions.

  • Explore renowned Batumi Botanical Garden;
  • Walk through Europe Square;
  • Discover romantic Piazza Square;
  • See the moving Ali and Nino statue;
  • Stroll along Batumi Boulevard;
  • Enjoy stunning Black Sea vistas;
  • Optional cable car ride;
  • Overnight stay in Batumi.
  • Duration of the tour: 5-6 hours
  • Driving duration: 1 hour

Day 8: Batumi – Tbilisi

[+]

Enjoy a final morning by the Black Sea, taking in the serene coastal atmosphere of Batumi. Check out from your hotel and begin the scenic journey back to Tbilisi through Georgia's diverse landscapes. Arrive in the capital city where your Georgian adventure began, reflecting on the incredible variety of experiences from ancient monasteries and cave cities to coastal beauty and mountain vistas.

  • Final morning in Batumi;
  • Optional seaside walk;
  • Check out from hotel;
  • Scenic drive back to Tbilisi;
  • Journey through diverse landscapes;
  • Arrive in Tbilisi;
  • Check-in and overnight in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 5 hours

Day 9: Departure from Tbilisi

[+]

Complete your unforgettable 9-day journey through Georgia's best landmarks and experiences. Enjoy a final Georgian breakfast and take last moments to explore Tbilisi if time permits. Check out from your hotel and transfer to Tbilisi International Airport for your departure, carrying with you memories of Georgia's rich layers of history, culture, natural beauty, and the warm hospitality that makes this country so special.

  • Final Georgian breakfast;
  • Optional last-minute city exploration;
  • Hotel check-out;
  • Transfer to Tbilisi International Airport;
  • Departure from Georgia;
  • End of memorable 9-day adventure.
  • Duration: As per flight schedule
