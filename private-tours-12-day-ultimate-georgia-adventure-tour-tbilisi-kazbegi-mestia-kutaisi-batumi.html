Tour Overview

Embark on the ultimate Georgian adventure with this comprehensive 12-day journey through historic landmarks, breathtaking landscapes, and vibrant cities. Begin in Tbilisi, exploring the historic Old Town, iconic Narikala Fortress, and charming streets steeped in history. Head to Kazbegi for awe-inspiring Caucasus Mountain views and a visit to the Ananuri Complex, a masterpiece of medieval architecture. In Kutaisi, uncover the beauty of the UNESCO-listed Gelati Monastery and the magical Prometheus Cave. Journey to Svaneti, a land of ancient towers and stunning peaks, where the unique villages of Mestia and Ushguli await. Conclude your tour in Batumi, where lush Botanical Gardens, vibrant city squares, and the tranquil Black Sea coast provide the perfect ending to your journey. This itinerary offers an ideal balance of history, natural wonders, and cultural immersion, making it a must for travelers seeking to explore the heart of the Caucasus.

Tour Itinerary [+]

Day 1: Arrival and City Tour in Tbilisi

[+]

Begin your ultimate 12-day Georgian adventure with a warm welcome and comprehensive city tour of Tbilisi. Explore the vibrant capital's historic Old Town, visit the iconic Narikala Fortress, and wander through charming streets steeped in history. This introductory day sets the perfect stage for your extensive journey through Georgia's most captivating regions, from mountain peaks to coastal beauty.

  • Arrival in Tbilisi and airport transfer;
  • Comprehensive city tour of historic Old Town;
  • Visit iconic Narikala Fortress;
  • Explore charming historic streets;
  • Introduction to Georgian culture and heritage;
  • First taste of Georgian hospitality;
  • Check-in and overnight in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 4-5 hours

Day 2: Tbilisi – Ananuri – Kazbegi

[+]

Journey from Tbilisi through stunning mountain landscapes to reach the breathtaking region of Kazbegi. Visit the magnificent Ananuri Complex, a masterpiece of medieval architecture beside the Aragvi River. This scenic drive through the Caucasus Mountains offers spectacular views and introduces you to Georgia's dramatic highland beauty.

  • Departure from Tbilisi to mountain regions;
  • Visit magnificent Ananuri Complex;
  • Experience masterpiece of medieval architecture;
  • Scenic drive through Caucasus Mountains;
  • Spectacular mountain views and landscapes;
  • Arrival in breathtaking Kazbegi region;
  • Check-in and overnight in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours

Day 3: Kazbegi

[+]

Immerse yourself fully in the awe-inspiring Caucasus Mountain views of Kazbegi. Visit the famous Gergeti Trinity Church and experience the breathtaking highland scenery that makes this region one of Georgia's most spectacular destinations. This day offers unparalleled mountain vistas and spiritual experiences in one of the country's most dramatic settings.

  • Full day exploring magnificent Kazbegi region;
  • Visit famous Gergeti Trinity Church;
  • Experience awe-inspiring Caucasus Mountain views;
  • Breathtaking highland scenery exploration;
  • Photography opportunities in dramatic landscapes;
  • Spiritual experiences in mountain settings;
  • Overnight stay in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours

Day 4: Kazbegi – Stalin Museum in Gori – Uplistsikhe – Tbilisi

[+]

Travel from the mountains to explore Georgia's fascinating historical layers. Visit the Stalin Museum in Gori to learn about this significant figure in world history, then explore the remarkable ancient rock-hewn city of Uplistsikhe, one of Georgia's most important archaeological sites. This day showcases the diverse cultural heritage that defines Georgian history.

  • Departure from Kazbegi mountain region;
  • Visit Stalin Museum in historic Gori;
  • Learn about significant world history;
  • Explore remarkable Uplistsikhe cave city;
  • Discover important archaeological heritage;
  • Experience diverse Georgian cultural layers;
  • Return to Tbilisi and overnight.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 4 hours total

Day 5: Tbilisi - Gelati - Motsameta - Kutaisi

[+]

Journey to western Georgia to explore one of the country's most historic regions. Visit the UNESCO-listed Gelati Monastery, showcasing the beauty of medieval Georgian architecture, and the serene Motsameta Monastery. These magnificent religious sites represent centuries of Georgian spiritual heritage and architectural achievement in stunning natural settings.

  • Travel to western Georgia and historic Kutaisi;
  • Visit UNESCO-listed Gelati Monastery;
  • Experience beauty of medieval architecture;
  • Explore serene Motsameta Monastery;
  • Discover Georgian spiritual heritage;
  • Learn about architectural achievements;
  • Check-in and overnight in Kutaisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 4.5 hours

Day 6: Kutaisi – Prometheus Cave – Martvili Canyon – Kutaisi

[+]

Discover the magical natural wonders of western Georgia with visits to stunning underground and surface attractions. Explore the magnificent Prometheus Cave with its spectacular formations, then experience the beauty of Martvili Canyon with its turquoise waters and dramatic landscapes. These natural wonders showcase Georgia's diverse geological treasures.

  • Explore magnificent Prometheus Cave;
  • Discover spectacular underground formations;
  • Experience magical underground chambers;
  • Visit beautiful Martvili Canyon;
  • Enjoy turquoise waters and canyon views;
  • Discover diverse geological treasures;
  • Return to Kutaisi for overnight.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours

Day 7: Kutaisi - Mestia

[+]

Journey to Svaneti, a land of ancient towers and stunning peaks, where the unique highland culture awaits. Travel to Mestia, a highland town famous for its distinctive Svan towers and mountain landscapes. This scenic journey takes you into one of Georgia's most remote and culturally distinct regions, where ancient traditions are perfectly preserved.

  • Journey to Svaneti highland region;
  • Travel to historic Mestia town;
  • Experience land of ancient towers;
  • Discover stunning mountain peaks;
  • Enter unique highland cultural region;
  • See distinctive Svan towers;
  • Check-in and overnight in Mestia.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 5 hours

Day 8: Mestia – Ushguli – Mestia

[+]

Explore the unique villages of Mestia and the UNESCO World Heritage site of Ushguli, one of Europe's highest permanently inhabited settlements. Experience the distinctive Svan culture, visit the Svaneti Museum, and marvel at the ancient defensive towers that dot this remarkable highland landscape. This day offers deep immersion into Georgia's most preserved mountain culture.

  • Visit UNESCO World Heritage Ushguli;
  • Explore Europe's highest inhabited settlement;
  • Experience distinctive Svan culture;
  • Visit informative Svaneti Museum;
  • Marvel at ancient defensive towers;
  • Deep cultural immersion experience;
  • Return to Mestia for overnight.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours round trip

Day 9: Mestia – Batumi Botanical Garden – Batumi

[+]

Travel from the highlands to Georgia's stunning Black Sea coast and the vibrant coastal city of Batumi. Visit the world-renowned Batumi Botanical Garden featuring diverse plant collections in spectacular coastal settings. This journey from mountains to sea showcases Georgia's incredible geographic and climatic diversity.

  • Travel from highlands to Black Sea coast;
  • Journey to vibrant coastal city Batumi;
  • Visit world-renowned Batumi Botanical Garden;
  • Explore diverse plant collections;
  • Experience spectacular coastal settings;
  • Discover geographic and climatic diversity;
  • Check-in and overnight in Batumi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 5 hours

Day 10: Batumi City Tour, Free Evening

[+]

Discover the charm and attractions of coastal Batumi with a comprehensive city tour. Explore the vibrant city squares, experience the unique blend of modern and traditional architecture, and enjoy the tranquil Black Sea coast atmosphere. A free evening allows you to relax and explore at your own pace, whether strolling the seaside or experiencing local nightlife.

  • Comprehensive Batumi city tour;
  • Explore vibrant city squares;
  • Experience unique architectural blend;
  • Discover modern and traditional elements;
  • Enjoy tranquil Black Sea coast;
  • Free evening for personal exploration;
  • Overnight stay in Batumi.
  • Duration of the tour: 4-5 hours, then free time

Day 11: Batumi – Tbilisi (Train)

[+]

Experience a scenic and comfortable train journey from the Black Sea coast back to Georgia's capital. The relaxing train ride offers beautiful views of Georgian landscapes as you travel through diverse terrain. This comfortable journey provides time to reflect on your comprehensive Georgian adventure while enjoying the changing scenery.

  • Scenic train journey from coast to capital;
  • Comfortable and relaxing rail travel;
  • Beautiful views of Georgian landscapes;
  • Journey through diverse terrain;
  • Time to reflect on adventures;
  • Arrival back in Tbilisi;
  • Final overnight in Tbilisi.
  • Duration: Full day train journey

Day 12: Departure from Tbilisi

[+]

Conclude your ultimate 12-day Georgian adventure with final moments in Tbilisi. Enjoy a leisurely breakfast and take time for any last-minute shopping or exploration. Transfer to Tbilisi International Airport for your departure, carrying with you unforgettable memories of Georgia's historic landmarks, breathtaking landscapes, vibrant cities, ancient culture, and the perfect ending to your comprehensive journey through the heart of the Caucasus.

  • Final leisurely breakfast in Tbilisi;
  • Last-minute shopping or exploration;
  • Hotel check-out and airport transfer;
  • Departure from Tbilisi International Airport;
  • End of ultimate 12-day Georgian adventure;
  • Memories of comprehensive Caucasus journey.
  • Duration: As per flight schedule
