Tour Overview

Begin your Georgian adventure in the vibrant city of Kutaisi, where history and natural wonders collide. Explore the sacred halls of the UNESCO-listed Gelati Monastery, venture underground into the mesmerizing Prometheus Cave, and sail through the tranquil waters of Martvili Canyon. Journey onward to Batumi, Georgia's Black Sea gem, offering a perfect blend of coastal beauty and modern allure. In Tbilisi, uncover centuries of history as you stroll through its winding streets, then head north to the dramatic landscapes of Kazbegi, where the iconic Gergeti Trinity Church stands against the backdrop of rugged peaks. This carefully crafted 11-day itinerary invites you to experience the heart and soul of Georgia through its most unforgettable sights, from ancient landmarks to awe-inspiring natural scenery.

Tour Itinerary [+]

Day 1: Arrival in Kutaisi

[+]

Begin your comprehensive 11-day Georgian grand tour in the historic city of Kutaisi, one of Georgia's oldest cities with over 3,000 years of rich cultural heritage. Arrive at Kutaisi International Airport and transfer to your accommodation in this charming ancient capital of western Georgia. Settle in and enjoy a welcome dinner featuring authentic Georgian cuisine, preparing for your extensive exploration of the country's most spectacular destinations.

  • Arrival at Kutaisi International Airport;
  • Transfer to accommodation in historic Kutaisi;
  • Introduction to 3,000-year Georgian heritage;
  • Ancient western Georgian capital discovery;
  • Welcome dinner with authentic cuisine;
  • Preparation for extensive country exploration;
  • Check-in and overnight in Kutaisi.
  • Duration: As per arrival schedule

Day 2: Kutaisi City Tour – Gelati Monastery – Motsameta Monastery

[+]

Explore Kutaisi's magnificent spiritual heritage with comprehensive city tour and monastery visits. Discover the UNESCO World Heritage Gelati Monastery, a masterpiece of medieval Georgian architecture representing the golden age of Georgian culture and learning. Continue to the serene Motsameta Monastery, dramatically positioned above the Tskhaltsitela River canyon, offering both spiritual significance and stunning natural beauty in peaceful surroundings.

  • Comprehensive Kutaisi city exploration;
  • UNESCO World Heritage Gelati Monastery;
  • Medieval Georgian architectural masterpiece;
  • Golden age of culture and learning;
  • Serene Motsameta Monastery visit;
  • Dramatic river canyon positioning;
  • Overnight stay in Kutaisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 1.5 hours

Day 3: Kutaisi – Prometheus Cave – Martvili Canyon – Kutaisi

[+]

Experience two of Georgia's most spectacular natural wonders today. Begin with the magnificent Prometheus Cave, featuring impressive stalactites, stalagmites, and underground rivers across 11 kilometers of accessible chambers. Continue to the picturesque Martvili Canyon, where you'll enjoy serene boat trips among lush greenery and dramatic limestone canyon walls, experiencing the perfect combination of geological marvels and natural beauty.

  • Magnificent Prometheus Cave exploration;
  • Impressive stalactites and stalagmites;
  • 11-kilometer underground river system;
  • Picturesque Martvili Canyon discovery;
  • Serene boat trips among lush greenery;
  • Dramatic limestone canyon walls;
  • Return to Kutaisi for overnight.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours

Day 4: Kutaisi – Batumi

[+]

Journey from the historic inland city of Kutaisi to Georgia's stunning Black Sea coast and the vibrant city of Batumi. Travel through diverse Georgian landscapes, experiencing the country's remarkable geographic variety from ancient cities to modern coastal resorts. Arrive in Batumi, Georgia's coastal gem offering a perfect blend of nature, culture, and seaside charm with its unique architectural fusion and lively atmosphere.

  • Journey from historic Kutaisi to coast;
  • Travel to stunning Black Sea Batumi;
  • Experience diverse Georgian landscapes;
  • Remarkable geographic variety discovery;
  • Ancient cities to modern coastal contrast;
  • Georgia's coastal gem with nature blend;
  • Check-in and overnight in Batumi.
  • Duration of the tour: 4-5 hours
  • Driving duration: 2.5 hours

Day 5: Batumi City Tour and Botanical Garden

[+]

Explore Batumi's captivating coastal charm with comprehensive city tour and botanical garden visit. Discover Piazza Square with its Italian-inspired architecture, stroll along the famous Batumi Boulevard seaside promenade, and witness the moving Ali and Nino statue representing eternal love. Visit the world-renowned Batumi Botanical Garden with its diverse plant collections from around the globe, set in spectacular coastal settings overlooking the Black Sea.

  • Comprehensive Batumi coastal exploration;
  • Piazza Square Italian-inspired architecture;
  • Famous Batumi Boulevard seaside promenade;
  • Moving Ali and Nino eternal love statue;
  • World-renowned Botanical Garden visit;
  • Diverse global plant collections;
  • Overnight stay in Batumi.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 1 hour

Day 6: Batumi – Tbilisi (Train)

[+]

Experience a scenic and comfortable train journey from the Black Sea coast to Georgia's vibrant capital city. The relaxing rail travel offers beautiful views of Georgian landscapes as you journey from the coastal region through diverse terrain toward Tbilisi. This comfortable journey provides time to reflect on your coastal experiences while anticipating the historical treasures awaiting in the capital city.

  • Scenic train journey from coast to capital;
  • Comfortable rail travel experience;
  • Beautiful Georgian landscape views;
  • Coastal to diverse terrain transition;
  • Time for coastal experience reflection;
  • Anticipation of capital historical treasures;
  • Arrival and overnight in Tbilisi.
  • Duration: Full day train journey

Day 7: Tbilisi City Tour

[+]

Discover Georgia's vibrant capital with comprehensive city exploration of Tbilisi's rich historical landmarks and modern attractions. Explore the historic Old Town with its narrow cobblestone streets, visit the ancient Narikala Fortress overlooking the city, and discover the famous sulfur baths district. Stroll along prestigious Rustaveli Avenue and experience the dynamic culture that perfectly blends ancient history with contemporary Georgian life. Enjoy dinner celebrating your Tbilisi discovery.

  • Comprehensive Tbilisi capital exploration;
  • Historic Old Town cobblestone streets;
  • Ancient Narikala Fortress overlooking city;
  • Famous sulfur baths district discovery;
  • Prestigious Rustaveli Avenue stroll;
  • Dynamic ancient and contemporary blend;
  • Dinner celebrating Tbilisi discovery.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 1 hour

Day 8: Tbilisi – Mtskheta – Ananuri – Kazbegi

[+]

Journey through Georgia's spiritual heart and scenic mountain landscapes toward dramatic Caucasus peaks. Visit Mtskheta, the ancient capital and UNESCO World Heritage Site, exploring Jvari Monastery and Svetitskhoveli Cathedral. Stop at the historic Ananuri Fortress overlooking the Zhinvali Reservoir, offering glimpses into Georgia's medieval past, before continuing along the scenic Georgian Military Highway to Kazbegi with its breathtaking mountain vistas.

  • Journey through spiritual heart Mtskheta;
  • UNESCO World Heritage ancient capital;
  • Jvari Monastery and Svetitskhoveli Cathedral;
  • Historic Ananuri Fortress at reservoir;
  • Georgia's medieval past glimpses;
  • Scenic Georgian Military Highway;
  • Check-in and overnight in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 4 hours

Day 9: Kazbegi

[+]

Immerse yourself in the spectacular mountain beauty of Kazbegi with full day exploration. Visit the iconic Gergeti Trinity Church, dramatically perched at 2,170 meters with breathtaking panoramic views of Mount Kazbek and surrounding Caucasus peaks. Experience the raw natural beauty of this high-altitude sanctuary where Georgian Orthodox spirituality meets the majestic power of the mountains, creating one of Georgia's most unforgettable experiences.

  • Full day spectacular Kazbegi exploration;
  • Iconic Gergeti Trinity Church visit;
  • 2,170-meter dramatic mountain perch;
  • Breathtaking Mount Kazbek panorama;
  • Surrounding Caucasus peaks views;
  • Orthodox spirituality meets mountain majesty;
  • Overnight stay in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours

Day 10: Kazbegi – Gori – Uplistsikhe – Kutaisi

[+]

Travel from the dramatic mountains to explore Georgia's fascinating historical layers and archaeological heritage. Visit Gori and the Stalin Museum, providing insight into the complex legacy of this controversial historical figure who was born here. Continue to the ancient rock-hewn town of Uplistsikhe, showcasing Georgia's remarkable archaeological heritage with fascinating cave dwellings and historical structures representing Georgia's ancient Silk Road connections.

  • Travel from dramatic mountains to history;
  • Gori Stalin Museum complex legacy;
  • Controversial historical figure insights;
  • Ancient rock-hewn Uplistsikhe town;
  • Remarkable archaeological heritage;
  • Fascinating cave dwellings and structures;
  • Return to Kutaisi for final night.
  • Duration of the tour: 9-10 hours
  • Driving duration: 6 hours total

Day 11: Departure from Kutaisi

[+]

Conclude your comprehensive 11-day Georgian grand tour with departure from Kutaisi International Airport. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Georgia's complete spectrum of wonders: from UNESCO World Heritage monasteries and dramatic mountain landscapes to coastal charm and ancient archaeological sites. This extensive journey has provided the ultimate experience of Georgia's natural beauty, rich history, and cultural heritage, representing the very best this extraordinary country has to offer.

  • Final departure from grand tour adventure;
  • Transfer to Kutaisi International Airport;
  • Memories of complete Georgian spectrum;
  • UNESCO sites to dramatic mountains;
  • Coastal charm and archaeological wonders;
  • Ultimate Georgian natural and cultural experience;
  • End of extraordinary 11-day grand tour.
  • Duration: As per flight schedule
