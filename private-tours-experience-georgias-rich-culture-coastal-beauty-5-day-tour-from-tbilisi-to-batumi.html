Tour Overview

Embark on a captivating 5-day journey through Georgia, starting and ending in Tbilisi, the country's vibrant capital. Explore ancient landmarks, wander through mystical cave cities, and experience the unique charm of Georgia's western regions. Your adventure concludes in Batumi, a lively city on the Black Sea coast, celebrated for its lush botanical gardens, vibrant squares, and breathtaking sunsets over the sea. This tour is perfect for travelers seeking a harmonious blend of cultural discovery and coastal relaxation.

Tour Itinerary [+]

Day 1: Tbilisi – Jvari – Uplistsikhe – Gori – Kutaisi

[+]

Begin your Georgian adventure with a departure from Tbilisi towards the ancient landmarks of the country. Visit the iconic Jvari Monastery overlooking the confluence of two rivers, explore the fascinating ancient cave town of Uplistsikhe carved into rocky cliffs, and discover Gori with its famous Stalin Museum. Conclude your day in Kutaisi, one of Georgia's oldest cities.

  • Departure from Tbilisi;
  • Visit Jvari Monastery (UNESCO World Heritage Site);
  • Explore Uplistsikhe ancient cave town;
  • Visit Stalin Museum in Gori;
  • Arrive in Kutaisi and check-in to hotel;
  • Overnight stay in Kutaisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 4 hours

Day 2: Kutaisi – Motsameta – Gelati – Kutaisi

[+]

Discover the spiritual and cultural treasures around Kutaisi today. Visit the mystical Motsameta Monastery perched dramatically above the Tskhaltsitela River, and explore the magnificent Gelati Monastery, a UNESCO World Heritage Site representing the golden age of medieval Georgian culture. Experience the vibrant atmosphere of Kutaisi Green Bazaar.

  • Visit Motsameta Monastery;
  • Explore Gelati Monastery (UNESCO World Heritage Site);
  • Walk through Kutaisi Green Bazaar;
  • Free time to explore Kutaisi city center;
  • Overnight stay in Kutaisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 1.5 hours

Day 3: Kutaisi – Prometheus Cave – Musicians Park – Batumi

[+]

Journey towards the Black Sea coast today, but first marvel at one of Georgia's natural wonders. Explore the breathtaking Prometheus Cave with its stunning stalactites, stalagmites, and underground rivers. Continue through the scenic landscapes to reach Batumi, stopping at Musicians Park along the way. Arrive in the vibrant coastal city for your seaside adventure.

  • Check out from hotel in Kutaisi;
  • Visit Prometheus Cave Natural Monument;
  • Stop at Musicians Park;
  • Scenic drive towards the Black Sea;
  • Arrive in Batumi and check-in to hotel;
  • Free evening in Batumi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours

Day 4: Explore Batumi and its vicinities

[+]

Immerse yourself in the unique charm of Batumi today. Explore the beautiful Batumi Botanical Garden with its diverse plant collections, admire the architectural highlights of Europe Square, discover the romantic Piazza Square, and witness the moving Ali and Nino Statue. Enjoy the lush coastal atmosphere and modern attractions of this vibrant Black Sea city.

  • Visit Batumi Botanical Garden;
  • Explore Europe Square;
  • Walk through Piazza Square;
  • See the Ali and Nino Statue;
  • Free time for seaside relaxation;
  • Optional cable car ride for panoramic views;
  • Overnight stay in Batumi.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 1 hour

Day 5: Batumi – Tbilisi

[+]

Enjoy your final morning in Batumi, perhaps taking a last stroll along the Black Sea coast or revisiting your favorite spots. Check out from your hotel and begin the scenic journey back to Tbilisi, Georgia's capital. Arrive in Tbilisi where your memorable 5-day Georgian adventure concludes.

  • Final morning in Batumi;
  • Check out from hotel;
  • Scenic drive back to Tbilisi;
  • Arrive in Tbilisi;
  • Tour concludes in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 5 hours
