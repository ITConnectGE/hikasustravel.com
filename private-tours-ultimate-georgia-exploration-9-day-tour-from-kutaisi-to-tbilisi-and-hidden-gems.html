Tour Overview

Start your Georgian journey in Kutaisi, a city steeped in history, where the serene Gelati and Motsameta Monasteries await. Delve into the underground wonders of Prometheus Cave before heading to the lively streets of Tbilisi, a city that seamlessly blends ancient traditions with modern charm. Travel through the spiritual heart of Georgia in Mtskheta, admire the medieval architecture of Ananuri, and be captivated by the dramatic mountain vistas of Kazbegi, crowned by the iconic Gergeti Trinity Church. Unearth the secrets of the Uplistsikhe cave city, explore the life of a historic figure at the Stalin Museum in Gori, and wrap up your adventure with the ancient ruins of Nokalakevi and the serene beauty of Martvili Canyon. This 9-day tour offers a thoughtfully curated exploration of Georgia's most iconic landmarks and natural wonders, perfect for those seeking a well-rounded experience of the country.

Tour Itinerary [+]

Day 1: Arrival in Kutaisi

[+]

Begin your ultimate 9-day Georgian exploration in Kutaisi, one of Georgia's oldest cities with over 3,000 years of history. Arrive at Kutaisi International Airport and transfer to your accommodation in this charming historic city. Take time to settle in and prepare for your comprehensive journey through Georgia's most captivating landmarks and hidden gems.

  • Arrival at Kutaisi International Airport;
  • Transfer to accommodation;
  • Introduction to Kutaisi's 3,000-year history;
  • Settling in charming historic city;
  • Preparation for comprehensive exploration;
  • Rest and preparation for adventure ahead;
  • Check-in and overnight in Kutaisi.
  • Duration: As per arrival schedule

Day 2: Kutaisi City Tour – Gelati Monastery – Motsameta Monastery

[+]

Immerse yourself in Kutaisi's spiritual heritage with visits to two magnificent monasteries. Explore the UNESCO World Heritage Gelati Monastery, a masterpiece of medieval Georgian architecture representing the golden age of Georgian culture. Continue to the serene Motsameta Monastery, dramatically perched above the Tskhaltsitela River, offering both spiritual significance and stunning natural beauty.

  • Comprehensive Kutaisi city exploration;
  • UNESCO World Heritage Gelati Monastery;
  • Medieval Georgian architectural masterpiece;
  • Golden age of Georgian culture discovery;
  • Serene Motsameta Monastery visit;
  • Dramatic river canyon setting;
  • Overnight stay in Kutaisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 1.5 hours

Day 3: Kutaisi – Prometheus Cave – Tbilisi

[+]

Experience one of Georgia's most spectacular natural wonders at Prometheus Cave, featuring stunning stalactites, stalagmites, and underground rivers across 11 kilometers of accessible chambers. This geological marvel offers a mesmerizing underground journey before continuing to Tbilisi, Georgia's vibrant capital, where ancient history seamlessly blends with modern energy.

  • Spectacular Prometheus Cave exploration;
  • Stunning stalactites and stalagmites;
  • Underground rivers and chambers;
  • 11-kilometer geological marvel;
  • Mesmerizing underground journey;
  • Travel to vibrant capital Tbilisi;
  • Check-in and overnight in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 4.5 hours

Day 4: Tbilisi City Tour

[+]

Discover the dynamic soul of Tbilisi with a comprehensive city tour. Explore the historic Old Town with its narrow cobblestone streets, visit the ancient Narikala Fortress overlooking the city, discover the famous sulfur baths district, and stroll along Rustaveli Avenue. Experience the vibrant blend of ancient history, Soviet heritage, and modern Georgian life that makes Tbilisi so captivating.

  • Comprehensive Tbilisi city discovery;
  • Historic Old Town exploration;
  • Ancient Narikala Fortress visit;
  • Famous sulfur baths district;
  • Rustaveli Avenue stroll;
  • Vibrant blend of ancient and modern;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 1 hour

Day 5: Tbilisi – Mtskheta – Ananuri – Kazbegi

[+]

Journey through Georgia's spiritual heart in Mtskheta, the ancient capital and UNESCO World Heritage Site. Visit Jvari Monastery and Svetitskhoveli Cathedral before continuing to the picturesque Ananuri Fortress beside Jinvali Reservoir. Travel along the scenic Georgian Military Highway to reach Kazbegi, where dramatic Caucasus Mountains and Mount Kazbek create some of Georgia's most spectacular landscapes.

  • Visit spiritual heart Mtskheta;
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site exploration;
  • Jvari Monastery and Svetitskhoveli Cathedral;
  • Picturesque Ananuri Fortress;
  • Scenic Georgian Military Highway;
  • Dramatic Kazbegi mountain arrival;
  • Check-in and overnight in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 4 hours

Day 6: Kazbegi – Gergeti Trinity Church – Tbilisi

[+]

Experience the iconic Gergeti Trinity Church, Georgia's most photographed landmark, perched dramatically at 2,170 meters with breathtaking panoramic views of Mount Kazbek and the surrounding Caucasus peaks. This spiritual site offers unparalleled mountain vistas and represents the perfect harmony between Georgian Orthodox faith and natural beauty. Return to Tbilisi in the evening.

  • Visit iconic Gergeti Trinity Church;
  • Georgia's most photographed landmark;
  • Breathtaking Mount Kazbek panorama;
  • 2,170-meter altitude spiritual site;
  • Caucasus peaks surrounding views;
  • Perfect harmony of faith and nature;
  • Return to Tbilisi evening arrival.
  • Duration of the tour: 10-11 hours
  • Driving duration: 7 hours total

Day 7: Tbilisi – Uplistsikhe – Gori Stalin Museum – Kutaisi

[+]

Explore Georgia's fascinating historical layers with a visit to Uplistsikhe, the ancient rock-hewn city representing early Georgian civilization. These remarkable cave dwellings and tunnels carved into rocky cliffs showcase Georgia's ancient Silk Road heritage. Continue to Gori to visit the Stalin Museum, providing insight into the complex legacy of this controversial historical figure, before arriving in Kutaisi.

  • Ancient Uplistsikhe rock-hewn city;
  • Early Georgian civilization showcase;
  • Remarkable cave dwellings and tunnels;
  • Ancient Silk Road heritage;
  • Gori Stalin Museum visit;
  • Complex controversial historical legacy;
  • Arrival in historic Kutaisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 9-10 hours
  • Driving duration: 6 hours total

Day 8: Hidden Gems Day – Tbilisi – Nokalakevi – Martvili Canyon – Kutaisi

[+]

Discover Georgia's hidden archaeological and natural treasures on this special day. Explore the ancient Nokalakevi Archaeological Site, often called "Georgian Troy," featuring fascinating ruins of a major ancient fortress city. Continue to the stunning Martvili Canyon, where emerald waters flow through dramatic limestone gorges, offering boat trips and walking trails through pristine natural beauty. This day showcases Georgia's lesser-known gems.

  • Special hidden treasures exploration;
  • Ancient Nokalakevi Archaeological Site;
  • Georgian Troy with fortress city ruins;
  • Major ancient fortress significance;
  • Stunning Martvili Canyon discovery;
  • Emerald waters and limestone gorges;
  • Return to Kutaisi for overnight.
  • Duration of the tour: 10-11 hours
  • Driving duration: 8 hours total

Day 9: Departure from Kutaisi

[+]

Conclude your ultimate 9-day Georgian exploration with departure from Kutaisi International Airport. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Georgia's most iconic landmarks combined with hidden gems like Nokalakevi and Martvili Canyon. This thoughtfully curated journey has provided a comprehensive experience of Georgia's culture, history, natural wonders, and architectural treasures, from ancient monasteries to dramatic mountain vistas and pristine canyons.

  • Final departure from Georgian exploration;
  • Transfer to Kutaisi International Airport;
  • Memories of iconic landmarks and hidden gems;
  • Nokalakevi and Martvili Canyon treasures;
  • Comprehensive culture and history experience;
  • Natural wonders and architectural treasures;
  • End of 9-day ultimate Georgian adventure.
  • Duration: As per flight schedule
