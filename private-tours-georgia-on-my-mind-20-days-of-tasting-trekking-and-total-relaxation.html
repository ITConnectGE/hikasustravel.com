Tour Overview

Venture across Georgia on a 20-day adventure filled with cultural heritage, scenic landscapes, and culinary delights. Discover Tbilisi's vibrant charm, taste traditional wines in Kakheti, trek through the majestic mountains of Svaneti, and unwind on the Black Sea coast. This immersive journey offers the perfect balance of history, nature, and relaxation, giving you a true taste of Georgia's spirit. Explore Georgia's diverse regions and be captivated by its ancient monasteries, medieval architecture, and lush vineyards. Journey through Tbilisi's charming Old Town, venture to the wine-producing region of Kakheti for world-famous Qvevri winemaking, and head to the Caucasus Mountains with Kazbegi's towering peaks and iconic Gergeti Trinity Church. Marvel at the ancient Svan towers in the rugged mountains of Svaneti and relax in the coastal city of Batumi by the Black Sea.

Experience the unique charm of Abanotubani sulfur baths district and get your first taste of Georgian hospitality and culture.
