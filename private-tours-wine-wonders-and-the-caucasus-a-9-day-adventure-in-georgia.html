Tour Overview

Discover the essence of Georgia on this 9-day tour packed with unforgettable experiences. Start in Tbilisi's Old Town, exploring landmarks like the Narikala Fortress and Metekhi Church, before journeying to Mtskheta, home to UNESCO World Heritage treasures. In Kazbegi, take in breathtaking Caucasus Mountain views at the Gergeti Trinity Church and explore the serene beauty of the Truso Valley. Immerse yourself in the rich traditions of Georgian winemaking in Kakheti, with tastings at local wineries and a stroll through the charming town of Sighnaghi, known as the "City of Love." Conclude your adventure with a free day in Tbilisi, perfect for shopping, savoring local cuisine, or simply relaxing. This itinerary offers the ideal combination of breathtaking landscapes, historic landmarks, and culinary delights, ensuring an enriching and memorable travel experience in the heart of the Caucasus.

Tour Itinerary [+]

Day 1: Arrival in Tbilisi

[+]

Begin your 9-day wine, wonders, and Caucasus adventure with arrival in Tbilisi, Georgia's vibrant capital and cultural heart. Airport transfer to your hotel for check-in and relaxation. If time permits, take an optional evening stroll around the nearby areas to get your first taste of Georgian culture and hospitality. Prepare for an incredible journey ahead through Georgia's most captivating landscapes and cultural treasures.

  • Arrival in Tbilisi;
  • Airport pick-up and transfer to hotel;
  • Hotel check-in and relaxation;
  • Optional evening exploration;
  • Welcome briefing about the tour;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration: As per arrival schedule

Day 2: Tbilisi City Tour

[+]

Explore Tbilisi's Old Town and discover the rich heritage of Georgia's capital. Visit iconic landmarks like the ancient Narikala Fortress overlooking the city, the historic Metekhi Church with its dramatic clifftop location, and wander through the charming cobblestone streets that tell the story of Georgia's diverse cultural influences. Experience the unique blend of traditional Georgian architecture with modern elements that make Tbilisi so captivating.

  • Explore Tbilisi Old Town;
  • Visit ancient Narikala Fortress;
  • See historic Metekhi Church;
  • Walk through cobblestone streets;
  • Discover cultural landmarks;
  • Learn about Tbilisi's rich history;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 4-5 hours
  • Driving duration: 30 minutes

Day 3: Mtskheta and Ananuri

[+]

Journey to Mtskheta, the ancient capital and spiritual heart of Georgia. Explore this UNESCO World Heritage Site, home to treasures like Svetitskhoveli Cathedral and Jvari Monastery. Continue to the picturesque Ananuri Fortress, a stunning medieval architectural complex beside the Aragvi River with breathtaking mountain backdrops. This day showcases Georgia's rich religious heritage and medieval craftsmanship.

  • Visit Mtskheta, the ancient capital;
  • Explore UNESCO World Heritage treasures;
  • See Svetitskhoveli Cathedral;
  • Visit Jvari Monastery;
  • Discover Ananuri Fortress;
  • Enjoy mountain and river scenery;
  • Return to Tbilisi;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours

Day 4: Kazbegi Exploration

[+]

Take in breathtaking Caucasus Mountain views as you journey to Kazbegi. Visit the iconic Gergeti Trinity Church, perched dramatically at 2,170 meters altitude with unparalleled views of Mount Kazbek and the surrounding peaks. This spiritual site offers some of the most spectacular mountain scenery in Georgia and serves as a perfect introduction to the majestic Caucasus landscape.

  • Journey to scenic Kazbegi;
  • Visit iconic Gergeti Trinity Church;
  • Take in breathtaking Caucasus views;
  • Experience Mount Kazbek scenery;
  • Photography opportunities;
  • Explore mountain landscapes;
  • Check-in and overnight in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours

Day 5: Gudauri and Stepantsminda

[+]

Explore the serene beauty of the high Caucasus today. Visit Gudauri, a ski resort town offering panoramic mountain views and fresh alpine air. Continue exploring Stepantsminda and discover the tranquil Truso Valley with its pristine natural environment. Experience the peaceful mountain atmosphere and dramatic landscapes that make the Georgian Caucasus so memorable.

  • Visit Gudauri ski resort town;
  • Enjoy panoramic mountain views;
  • Explore Stepantsminda area;
  • Discover serene Truso Valley;
  • Experience pristine natural environment;
  • Alpine landscape photography;
  • Overnight stay in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 2 hours

Day 6: Kakheti Wine Region

[+]

Immerse yourself in the rich traditions of Georgian winemaking in the renowned Kakheti region. Experience tastings at local wineries and discover Georgia's 8,000-year-old wine heritage. Visit charming Sighnaghi, known as the "City of Love," with its cobblestone streets and stunning views over the Alazani Valley. This day celebrates Georgia's most famous cultural export - its exceptional wines.

  • Journey to renowned Kakheti wine region;
  • Experience tastings at local wineries;
  • Learn about 8,000-year wine heritage;
  • Visit charming Sighnaghi "City of Love";
  • Explore cobblestone streets;
  • Enjoy views over Alazani Valley;
  • Overnight stay in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 4 hours

Day 7: Telavi and Alaverdi Monastery

[+]

Explore Telavi, the administrative center of the Kakheti region, known for its historic sites and connection to Georgian wine culture. Visit the magnificent Alaverdi Monastery, one of the highest cathedrals in Georgia and an important center of Georgian Orthodox Christianity. This day combines cultural exploration with spiritual heritage in the heart of Georgia's wine country.

  • Explore Telavi, center of Kakheti region;
  • Visit historic sites and monuments;
  • See magnificent Alaverdi Monastery;
  • Learn about Georgian Orthodox heritage;
  • Experience one of Georgia's highest cathedrals;
  • Discover wine country culture;
  • Check-in and overnight in Telavi.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 1.5 hours

Day 8: Free Day in Tbilisi

[+]

Return to Tbilisi for a well-deserved free day, perfect for shopping, savoring local cuisine, or simply relaxing. Explore the city at your own pace, perhaps revisiting favorite spots from your arrival or discovering new areas you haven't yet explored. This flexible day allows you to enjoy Tbilisi's vibrant atmosphere, visit local markets, or treat yourself to a traditional Georgian feast.

  • Return journey to Tbilisi;
  • Free day for personal exploration;
  • Optional shopping and souvenir hunting;
  • Savor local cuisine at restaurants;
  • Visit local markets and cafes;
  • Relax and reflect on your journey;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: Free day
  • Driving duration: 2.5 hours (return to Tbilisi)

Day 9: Departure

[+]

Conclude your enriching and memorable 9-day travel experience in the heart of the Caucasus. Enjoy a final Georgian breakfast and take last moments to soak in the atmosphere of Tbilisi. Check out from your hotel and transfer to Tbilisi International Airport for your departure, carrying with you unforgettable memories of Georgia's breathtaking landscapes, historic landmarks, wine traditions, and culinary delights.

  • Final Georgian breakfast;
  • Last moments in Tbilisi;
  • Hotel check-out;
  • Transfer to Tbilisi International Airport;
  • Departure from Georgia;
  • End of 9-day Caucasus adventure.
  • Duration: As per flight schedule
