Tour Overview

Trace the pathways of Georgia's rich history and breathtaking landscapes, starting from the historic city of Kutaisi. Visit ancient monasteries, gaze upon the majestic Kazbegi Mountains, and explore the mysterious depths of Prometheus Cave. In the highlands of Svaneti, discover a world untouched by time, where unique traditions thrive amid awe-inspiring alpine vistas including the UNESCO World Heritage village of Ushguli, one of Europe's highest inhabited settlements. Feel the coastal charm of Batumi by the Black Sea, and embrace the lively spirit of Tbilisi, a city blending centuries-old heritage with contemporary allure. This comprehensive 13-day itinerary includes visits to UNESCO World Heritage sites, ancient cave towns, dramatic mountain landscapes, and pristine natural wonders. Designed for those who crave a perfect mix of cultural discovery and scenic beauty, this journey showcases the complete essence of Georgia with unforgettable experiences at every turn, from medieval Svan towers to Black Sea coastlines.

Tour Itinerary [+]

Day 1: Arrival in Kutaisi

[+]

Begin your grand 13-day cultural and scenic Georgian adventure in the historic city of Kutaisi, one of Georgia's oldest cities with over 3,000 years of rich heritage. Arrive at Kutaisi International Airport and transfer to your accommodation in this charming ancient capital of western Georgia. Settle in and prepare for an extensive exploration that will take you through Georgia's most spectacular destinations, from ancient monasteries to high mountain villages.

  • Arrival at Kutaisi International Airport;
  • Transfer to historic Kutaisi accommodation;
  • Introduction to 3,000-year Georgian heritage;
  • Ancient western Georgian capital discovery;
  • Preparation for extensive exploration;
  • Anticipation of spectacular destinations ahead;
  • Check-in and overnight in Kutaisi.
  • Duration: As per arrival schedule

Day 2: Kutaisi City Tour – Gelati Monastery – Motsameta Monastery

[+]

Explore Kutaisi's magnificent spiritual and cultural heritage with comprehensive city tour and visits to two of Georgia's most important monasteries. Discover the UNESCO World Heritage Gelati Monastery, a masterpiece of medieval Georgian architecture representing the golden age of Georgian culture and learning. Continue to the serene Motsameta Monastery, dramatically positioned above the Tskhaltsitela River, offering both spiritual significance and stunning natural beauty in peaceful surroundings.

  • Comprehensive Kutaisi city exploration;
  • UNESCO World Heritage Gelati Monastery;
  • Medieval Georgian architectural masterpiece;
  • Golden age of culture and learning;
  • Serene Motsameta Monastery visit;
  • Dramatic river canyon positioning;
  • Overnight stay in Kutaisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 1.5 hours

Day 3: Kutaisi – Prometheus Cave – Mestia

[+]

Experience Georgia's spectacular natural wonders before journeying to the remote highlands of Svaneti. Explore the magnificent Prometheus Cave with its impressive stalactites, stalagmites, and underground rivers across 11 kilometers of accessible chambers. Continue your journey to Mestia, the heart of Svaneti region, surrounded by breathtaking mountain views and known for its medieval Svan towers. Enter a world untouched by time where unique alpine traditions thrive.

  • Magnificent Prometheus Cave exploration;
  • Impressive stalactites and underground rivers;
  • 11-kilometer natural wonder;
  • Journey to remote Svaneti highlands;
  • Mestia heart of Svaneti discovery;
  • Medieval Svan towers introduction;
  • Check-in and overnight in Mestia.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 6 hours

Day 4: Mestia – Ushguli – Mestia

[+]

Discover one of Europe's most remarkable settlements with a day trip to Ushguli, one of the highest inhabited villages in Europe and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. This ancient village offers a glimpse into Georgia's pristine mountain culture and traditions, featuring medieval Svan towers and breathtaking alpine landscapes. Experience the timeless charm of this remote highland community where ancient Svan customs are perfectly preserved amid awe-inspiring mountain vistas.

  • Day trip to remarkable Ushguli village;
  • Highest inhabited European settlement;
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site exploration;
  • Pristine Georgian mountain culture;
  • Medieval Svan towers and landscapes;
  • Ancient customs perfectly preserved;
  • Return to Mestia for overnight.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours round trip

Day 5: Mestia – Martvili Canyon – Batumi

[+]

Journey from the high mountains of Svaneti to the stunning Black Sea coast, stopping to experience one of western Georgia's most beautiful natural wonders. Visit the picturesque Martvili Canyon, featuring serene boat trips amidst lush greenery and dramatic limestone canyon walls. Experience the remarkable geographic diversity as you travel from alpine peaks to coastal plains, arriving in Batumi, Georgia's vibrant coastal gem with its unique blend of nature and culture.

  • Journey from Svaneti mountains to coast;
  • Picturesque Martvili Canyon visit;
  • Serene boat trips among lush greenery;
  • Dramatic limestone canyon walls;
  • Remarkable geographic diversity experience;
  • Arrival in vibrant coastal Batumi;
  • Check-in and overnight in Batumi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 6 hours

Day 6: Batumi City Tour and Botanical Garden

[+]

Explore Batumi's captivating coastal charm and modern attractions with comprehensive city tour and botanical garden visit. Discover the elegant Piazza Square with its Italian-inspired architecture, stroll along the famous Batumi Boulevard seaside promenade, and witness the moving Ali and Nino statue representing eternal love. Visit the world-renowned Batumi Botanical Garden with its diverse plant collections from around the globe, set in spectacular coastal settings overlooking the Black Sea.

  • Comprehensive Batumi coastal exploration;
  • Elegant Piazza Square Italian architecture;
  • Famous Batumi Boulevard promenade;
  • Moving Ali and Nino eternal love statue;
  • World-renowned Botanical Garden;
  • Diverse global plant collections;
  • Overnight stay in Batumi.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 1 hour

Day 7: Batumi – Tbilisi (Train)

[+]

Experience a scenic and comfortable train journey from the Black Sea coast to Georgia's vibrant capital city. The relaxing rail travel offers beautiful views of Georgian landscapes as you journey from the coastal region through diverse terrain toward Tbilisi. This comfortable journey provides time to reflect on your mountain and coastal experiences while anticipating the historical and cultural treasures awaiting in Georgia's dynamic capital.

  • Scenic train journey coast to capital;
  • Comfortable rail travel experience;
  • Beautiful Georgian landscape views;
  • Coastal to diverse terrain transition;
  • Reflection on mountain and coastal experiences;
  • Anticipation of capital treasures;
  • Arrival and overnight in Tbilisi.
  • Duration: Full day train journey

Day 8: Tbilisi City Tour

[+]

Discover Georgia's vibrant capital with comprehensive city exploration of Tbilisi's rich historical landmarks and modern attractions. Explore the historic Old Town with its narrow cobblestone streets, visit the ancient Narikala Fortress overlooking the city, and discover the famous sulfur baths district. Stroll along prestigious Rustaveli Avenue and cross the modern Peace Bridge, experiencing the dynamic culture that perfectly blends centuries-old heritage with contemporary Georgian life.

  • Comprehensive Tbilisi capital exploration;
  • Historic Old Town cobblestone streets;
  • Ancient Narikala Fortress overlook;
  • Famous sulfur baths district discovery;
  • Prestigious Rustaveli Avenue and Peace Bridge;
  • Heritage and contemporary life blend;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 1 hour

Day 9: Free Day in Tbilisi

[+]

Enjoy a leisurely free day to explore Tbilisi at your own pace and discover the city's hidden gems. Revisit favorite spots from yesterday's tour, explore local markets and neighborhoods, relax in the famous sulfur baths, visit additional museums or galleries, or simply unwind in local cafes and restaurants. This flexible day allows you to experience Tbilisi like a local and enjoy the city's vibrant atmosphere at your own rhythm.

  • Leisurely free day personal exploration;
  • Discover city's hidden gems;
  • Revisit favorite spots or explore new areas;
  • Local markets and neighborhood exploration;
  • Optional sulfur baths or museums;
  • Experience Tbilisi like a local;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration: Free day for personal activities

Day 10: Tbilisi – Mtskheta – Ananuri – Kazbegi

[+]

Journey through Georgia's spiritual heart and scenic mountain landscapes toward the dramatic Caucasus peaks. Visit Mtskheta, one of the oldest cities in Georgia and UNESCO World Heritage Site, exploring the sacred Jvari Monastery and Svetitskhoveli Cathedral. Stop at the historic Ananuri Fortress overlooking the Zhinvali Reservoir, showcasing medieval architecture along the scenic Georgian Military Highway, before arriving in Kazbegi with its breathtaking mountain vistas.

  • Journey through spiritual heart Mtskheta;
  • UNESCO World Heritage oldest city;
  • Sacred Jvari Monastery and Cathedral;
  • Historic Ananuri Fortress at reservoir;
  • Medieval architecture on Military Highway;
  • Arrival in breathtaking Kazbegi;
  • Check-in and overnight in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 4 hours

Day 11: Kazbegi

[+]

Immerse yourself in the spectacular mountain beauty of Kazbegi with full day exploration of its dramatic landscapes and iconic landmarks. Visit the renowned Gergeti Trinity Church, dramatically perched at 2,170 meters with breathtaking panoramic views of Mount Kazbek and surrounding Caucasus peaks. Explore additional highlights including Gveleti Waterfalls and Dariali Gorge, experiencing the raw natural power and spiritual significance of this high-altitude mountain sanctuary.

  • Full day spectacular Kazbegi exploration;
  • Renowned Gergeti Trinity Church visit;
  • 2,170-meter dramatic mountain perch;
  • Mount Kazbek and Caucasus panorama;
  • Gveleti Waterfalls and Dariali Gorge;
  • Raw natural power and spiritual significance;
  • Overnight stay in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours

Day 12: Kazbegi – Gori – Uplistsikhe – Kutaisi

[+]

Travel from the dramatic mountains to explore Georgia's fascinating historical layers and archaeological heritage. Visit Gori and the Stalin Museum, providing insight into the complex legacy of this controversial historical figure who was born here. Continue to the ancient rock-hewn town of Uplistsikhe, showcasing Georgia's remarkable archaeological heritage with fascinating cave dwellings and historical structures reflecting Georgia's ancient connections to the Silk Road trade routes.

  • Travel from dramatic mountains to history;
  • Gori Stalin Museum complex legacy;
  • Controversial historical figure insights;
  • Ancient rock-hewn Uplistsikhe town;
  • Remarkable archaeological heritage;
  • Ancient Silk Road trade connections;
  • Return to Kutaisi for final night.
  • Duration of the tour: 9-10 hours
  • Driving duration: 6 hours total

Day 13: Departure from Kutaisi

[+]

Conclude your extraordinary 13-day Grand Georgia Adventure with departure from Kutaisi International Airport. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Georgia's complete cultural and scenic spectrum: from the UNESCO World Heritage monasteries and remote Svaneti highlands to coastal charm and dramatic mountain landscapes. This comprehensive journey has showcased the essence of Georgia with unique experiences at every turn, from medieval Svan towers to ancient cave cities, representing the ultimate Georgian cultural and scenic adventure.

  • Final departure from extraordinary adventure;
  • Transfer to Kutaisi International Airport;
  • Memories of complete cultural spectrum;
  • UNESCO sites and remote highlands;
  • Coastal charm to dramatic mountains;
  • Ultimate Georgian cultural adventure;
  • End of exceptional 13-day journey.
  • Duration: As per flight schedule
