Tour Overview

Immerse yourself in the captivating charm of Georgia on this 7-day cultural journey, beginning in the historic city of Kutaisi. Traverse the country's most iconic landmarks, from the ancient Gelati Monastery to the vibrant streets of Tbilisi. Experience the timeless beauty of the Kazbegi Mountains, where nature and history intertwine at sites like the Gergeti Trinity Church, perched against the backdrop of the towering Mount Kazbek. Along the way, explore the fascinating Uplistsikhe cave city, a relic of Georgia's ancient Silk Road heritage, and soak in the serene views of the Zhinvali Reservoir near the Ananuri Fortress. This carefully curated tour captures the essence of Georgia's culture, history, and natural splendor, promising an unforgettable adventure for travelers seeking to uncover the soul of this extraordinary destination.

Tour Itinerary [+]

Day 1: Arrival in Kutaisi

[+]

Begin your captivating 7-day cultural journey in the historic city of Kutaisi, Georgia's ancient western capital with over 3,000 years of rich history. Arrive at Kutaisi International Airport and transfer to your accommodation, where you'll start exploring this charming city known for its UNESCO World Heritage sites and peaceful atmosphere.

  • Arrival at Kutaisi International Airport;
  • Transfer to accommodation in historic Kutaisi;
  • Introduction to Georgia's ancient western capital;
  • Over 3,000 years of rich cultural heritage;
  • UNESCO World Heritage sites exploration;
  • Charming city with peaceful atmosphere;
  • Check-in and overnight in Kutaisi.
  • Duration: As per arrival schedule

Day 2: Kutaisi City Tour – Gelati Monastery – Motsameta Monastery

[+]

Explore Kutaisi's rich cultural heritage starting with the magnificent Gelati Monastery, a UNESCO World Heritage Site representing the golden age of medieval Georgian culture and architecture. Continue to the serene Motsameta Monastery, dramatically perched above a river canyon, offering stunning views and a peaceful spiritual atmosphere. Enjoy dinner in Kutaisi experiencing authentic Georgian hospitality.

  • Comprehensive Kutaisi city tour;
  • Visit magnificent Gelati Monastery;
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site exploration;
  • Medieval Georgian culture and architecture;
  • Serene Motsameta Monastery visit;
  • Stunning canyon views and atmosphere;
  • Dinner in Kutaisi with Georgian hospitality.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours

Day 3: Kutaisi – Prometheus Cave – Tbilisi

[+]

Experience the spectacular Prometheus Cave, a natural wonder with impressive stalactites and stalagmites located near Kutaisi. Explore the underground chambers and geological formations that span 11 kilometers, making this a must-see for nature enthusiasts. After the cave visit, travel to Georgia's vibrant capital Tbilisi for the next part of your cultural adventure.

  • Spectacular Prometheus Cave exploration;
  • Impressive stalactites and stalagmites;
  • Underground chambers and formations;
  • 11-kilometer geological wonder;
  • Must-see natural attraction;
  • Travel to vibrant capital Tbilisi;
  • Check-in and overnight in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 4.5 hours

Day 4: Tbilisi City Tour – Narikala Fortress – Old Town

[+]

Discover Georgia's vibrant capital Tbilisi, filled with historical landmarks like the iconic Narikala Fortress and the charming Old Town with its narrow cobblestone streets. Explore the dynamic culture of the city, visit the famous sulfur baths, and stroll along Rustaveli Avenue. Experience the perfect blend of ancient history and modern vibrancy. Enjoy dinner in Tbilisi celebrating Georgian cuisine and hospitality.

  • Comprehensive Tbilisi city exploration;
  • Iconic Narikala Fortress visit;
  • Charming Old Town with cobblestone streets;
  • Dynamic culture and historic landmarks;
  • Famous sulfur baths experience;
  • Rustaveli Avenue stroll;
  • Dinner celebrating Georgian cuisine.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours

Day 5: Tbilisi – Ananuri Fortress – Kazbegi – Gergeti Trinity Church – Tbilisi

[+]

Experience the timeless beauty of the Kazbegi Mountains along the scenic Georgian Military Highway. Stop at the historic Ananuri Fortress overlooking the Zhinvali Reservoir, offering glimpses into Georgia's medieval past. Continue to Kazbegi (Stepantsminda), famous for dramatic mountain landscapes and the iconic Gergeti Trinity Church, perched on a hill with panoramic views of Mount Kazbek. Return to Tbilisi in the evening.

  • Scenic Georgian Military Highway journey;
  • Historic Ananuri Fortress exploration;
  • Zhinvali Reservoir panoramic views;
  • Georgia's medieval past discovery;
  • Dramatic Kazbegi mountain landscapes;
  • Iconic Gergeti Trinity Church visit;
  • Return to Tbilisi evening arrival.
  • Duration of the tour: 10-11 hours
  • Driving duration: 7 hours total

Day 6: Tbilisi – Uplistsikhe Cave City – Gori Stalin Museum – Kutaisi

[+]

Explore the fascinating Uplistsikhe cave city, an ancient rock-hewn town showcasing Georgia's archaeological heritage with remarkable cave dwellings and historical structures from Georgia's ancient Silk Road heritage. Continue to Gori to visit the Stalin Museum, providing insight into the life of one of the most controversial figures in history, Joseph Stalin, who was born here. Return to Kutaisi for your final night.

  • Fascinating Uplistsikhe cave city exploration;
  • Ancient rock-hewn archaeological heritage;
  • Remarkable cave dwellings and structures;
  • Georgia's ancient Silk Road heritage;
  • Stalin Museum visit in Gori;
  • Controversial historical figure insights;
  • Return to Kutaisi for final night.
  • Duration of the tour: 9-10 hours
  • Driving duration: 6 hours total

Day 7: Departure from Kutaisi

[+]

Conclude your captivating 7-day cultural journey through Georgia with departure from Kutaisi International Airport. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Georgia's cultural wonders, from ancient UNESCO monasteries and cave cities to vibrant capital streets and dramatic mountain landscapes. This carefully curated adventure has captured the essence of Georgia's culture, history, and natural splendor, promising memories of an extraordinary destination.

  • Final departure from cultural journey;
  • Transfer to Kutaisi International Airport;
  • Memories of Georgia's cultural wonders;
  • UNESCO monasteries and cave cities;
  • Vibrant capital and mountain landscapes;
  • Essence of culture, history, and nature;
  • End of extraordinary 7-day adventure.
  • Duration: As per flight schedule
