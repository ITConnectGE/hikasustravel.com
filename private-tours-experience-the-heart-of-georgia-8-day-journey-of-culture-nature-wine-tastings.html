Tour Overview

Immerse yourself in the best of Georgia's cultural heritage, breathtaking landscapes, and celebrated wine traditions on this 8-day tour. Tailored for history buffs, nature enthusiasts, and wine lovers, this itinerary offers a perfect blend of discovery and indulgence in the heart of the Caucasus. Traverse historic landmarks, marvel at scenic vistas, and enjoy world-class wine tastings that showcase Georgia's ancient winemaking traditions. From ancient cave cities and UNESCO World Heritage Sites to lush vineyards and vibrant cities, this tour captures the essence of Georgia's diversity. Whether exploring the cobblestone streets of Tbilisi, gazing at the majestic Caucasus Mountains, or savoring the finest local cuisine, every moment is crafted for a truly unforgettable travel experience.

Tour Itinerary [+]

Day 1: Arrival and City Tour in Tbilisi

[+]

Begin your Georgian adventure with arrival in Tbilisi, the vibrant capital that beautifully blends ancient history with modern life. Explore the historic Old Town with its narrow cobblestone streets, visit the iconic Holy Trinity Cathedral, and admire the ancient Narikala Fortress overlooking the city. Discover the architectural beauty of the Peace Bridge and immerse yourself in the unique atmosphere of this captivating Caucasus capital.

  • Arrival in Tbilisi and hotel check-in;
  • Explore Tbilisi Old Town;
  • Visit Holy Trinity Cathedral;
  • See Narikala Fortress;
  • Walk across Peace Bridge;
  • Discover local culture and cuisine;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 4-5 hours
  • Driving duration: 30 minutes

Day 2: Tbilisi – Jvari – Mtskheta – Ananuri – Kazbegi

[+]

Journey along the famous Georgian Military Highway towards the majestic Caucasus Mountains. Visit the UNESCO World Heritage sites of Jvari Monastery and Svetitskhoveli Cathedral in Mtskheta, Georgia's spiritual heart. Stop at the picturesque Ananuri Fortress beside the stunning Jinvali Reservoir, then continue to Stepantsminda (Kazbegi), where dramatic mountain landscapes await.

  • Visit Jvari Monastery (UNESCO World Heritage Site);
  • Explore Mtskheta and Svetitskhoveli Cathedral;
  • Stop at Ananuri Architectural Complex;
  • Enjoy views of Jinvali Reservoir;
  • Arrive in Stepantsminda (Kazbegi);
  • Overnight stay in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 3.5 hours

Day 3: Kazbegi – Gergeti Trinity Church – Gveleti Waterfall – Kazbegi

[+]

Experience the natural wonders of the Caucasus Mountains today. Visit the iconic Gergeti Trinity Church, one of Georgia's most photographed landmarks, offering breathtaking panoramic views of Mount Kazbek and the surrounding peaks. Continue to the stunning Gveleti Waterfall, where you can enjoy the pristine mountain nature and crystal-clear waters cascading from the heights.

  • Visit Gergeti Trinity Church;
  • Enjoy panoramic views of Mount Kazbek;
  • Hike to Gveleti Waterfall;
  • Experience pristine mountain nature;
  • Photography opportunities;
  • Return to Kazbegi;
  • Overnight stay in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 1 hour

Day 4: Kazbegi – Uplistsikhe – Gori – Tbilisi

[+]

Journey back towards Tbilisi, stopping to explore Georgia's fascinating archaeological heritage. Visit Uplistsikhe, the ancient rock-hewn town that showcases early Georgian civilization with its remarkable cave dwellings and tunnels carved into the rocky cliffs. Continue to Gori to visit the Stalin Museum, learning about this controversial historical figure, before returning to Tbilisi.

  • Depart Kazbegi;
  • Explore Uplistsikhe ancient cave town;
  • Visit Stalin Museum in Gori;
  • Learn about Georgian archaeological heritage;
  • Return to Tbilisi;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 9-10 hours
  • Driving duration: 5 hours

Day 5: Tbilisi – Telavi – Lunch and Wine Tasting at the Winery – Tsinandali – Telavi

[+]

Enter Georgia's renowned wine country today as you journey to the heart of the Kakheti region. Arrive in Telavi and enjoy lunch with wine tasting at a local winery, experiencing traditional Georgian winemaking techniques. Visit the historic Tsinandali Estate with its beautiful gardens and learn about Georgia's ancient wine culture, dating back 8,000 years.

  • Travel to Kakheti wine region;
  • Arrive in Telavi;
  • Lunch and wine tasting at local winery;
  • Visit Tsinandali Estate and gardens;
  • Learn about Georgian wine traditions;
  • Overnight stay in Telavi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 2.5 hours

Day 6: Telavi – Sighnaghi – Bodbe – Dinner and Wine Tasting at the Winery – Tbilisi

[+]

Explore more gems of the Kakheti region today. Visit Sighnaghi, the charming "City of Love" with its cobblestone streets and stunning views over the Alazani Valley. Continue to Bodbe Monastery, a revered site in Georgian Orthodox Christianity and the final resting place of St. Nino. End your wine region exploration with dinner and wine tasting at a local winery before returning to Tbilisi.

  • Visit Sighnaghi, the "City of Love";
  • Explore Bodbe Monastery;
  • Learn about St. Nino's legacy;
  • Enjoy dinner and wine tasting;
  • Experience Kakheti wine traditions;
  • Return to Tbilisi;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 9-10 hours
  • Driving duration: 3.5 hours

Day 7: Free Day in Tbilisi

[+]

Enjoy a leisurely free day to explore Tbilisi at your own pace. Revisit your favorite spots from the first day, discover hidden gems in the city's diverse neighborhoods, relax in the famous sulfur baths, explore local markets, or simply unwind and reflect on your incredible Georgian journey. This is your time to experience Tbilisi like a local.

  • Free day for personal exploration;
  • Optional sulfur baths experience;
  • Explore local markets and neighborhoods;
  • Visit additional museums or galleries;
  • Shop for Georgian souvenirs;
  • Enjoy local cafes and restaurants;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.

Day 8: Tbilisi – Departure

[+]

Enjoy your final Georgian breakfast and take some last moments to soak in the atmosphere of Tbilisi. Check out from your hotel and transfer to Tbilisi International Airport for your departure, carrying with you unforgettable memories of Georgia's cultural heritage, stunning landscapes, mountain adventures, and exceptional wine traditions.

  • Final Georgian breakfast;
  • Hotel check-out;
  • Transfer to Tbilisi International Airport;
  • Departure from Georgia;
  • End of memorable 8-day journey.
  • Duration: As per flight schedule
