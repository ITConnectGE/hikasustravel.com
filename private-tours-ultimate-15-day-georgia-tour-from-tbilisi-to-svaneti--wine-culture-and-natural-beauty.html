Tour Overview

Embark on a 15-day adventure through Georgia, a land where ancient history, breathtaking landscapes, and rich traditions come together. Experience a blend of cultural heritage, scenic mountains, lush vineyards, and the tranquil Black Sea coast. Head to the charming towns of Sighnaghi and Telavi, renowned for their exceptional wineries and Qvevri wine making excursion. Soak in the stunning landscapes of Kazbegi, walk through the ancient rock-hewn streets of Uplistsikhe, and admire the UNESCO-protected Gelati Monastery in Kutaisi. Be mesmerized by the surreal formations of Prometheus Cave and continue to the remote mountain region of Svaneti, where the timeless villages of Mestia and Ushguli await. End your adventure in Batumi, where the Botanical Garden, lively squares, and serene Black Sea coast provide the perfect setting for relaxation. Conclude your trip with a picturesque train ride back to Tbilisi, soaking in the last glimpses of Georgia's diverse landscapes.

Tour Itinerary [+]

Day 1: Arrival and City Tour in Tbilisi

[+]

Begin your ultimate 15-day Georgian adventure with arrival and comprehensive city tour of Tbilisi. Explore the historic Old Town, visit the iconic Narikala Fortress, discover sulfur baths, and see modern landmarks like Rustaveli Avenue and Peace Bridge. This extensive introduction sets the perfect foundation for your journey through Georgia's ancient history, breathtaking landscapes, and rich traditions.

  • Arrival in Tbilisi and airport transfer;
  • Comprehensive historic Old Town exploration;
  • Visit iconic Narikala Fortress;
  • Discover traditional sulfur baths;
  • See modern landmarks and Peace Bridge;
  • Introduction to Georgian history and culture;
  • Check-in and overnight in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 4-5 hours

Day 2: Tbilisi – Bodbe – Sighnaghi – Khareba Winery – Telavi

[+]

Journey to the charming "City of Love" Sighnaghi with its cobblestone streets overlooking the Alazani Valley. Visit the Bodbe Monastery and experience the renowned Khareba Winery for exceptional wine tasting. Continue to Telavi, the heart of the Kakheti wine region, beginning your immersion into Georgia's world-class wine culture and traditions.

  • Journey to Kakheti wine region;
  • Visit historic Bodbe Monastery;
  • Explore charming Sighnaghi "City of Love";
  • Experience renowned Khareba Winery;
  • Exceptional wine tasting session;
  • Continue to Telavi wine capital;
  • Check-in and overnight in Telavi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours

Day 3: Telavi – Tsinandali – Local Winery – Vardisubani – Telavi

[+]

Explore the famous Tsinandali Estate and participate in a traditional Qvevri wine making excursion. Visit local wineries and discover the historic Batonistsikhe castle complex. Experience Vardisubani's wine culture and deepen your understanding of Georgia's 8,000-year-old winemaking traditions in the heart of Kakheti region.

  • Visit famous Tsinandali Estate;
  • Participate in traditional Qvevri wine making;
  • Experience ancient winemaking techniques;
  • Visit local wineries for tastings;
  • Explore historic Batonistsikhe castle;
  • Discover Vardisubani wine culture;
  • Return to Telavi for overnight.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 2 hours

Day 4: Telavi – Tbilisi, Free Evening

[+]

Return to Tbilisi after your wine region exploration and enjoy a free evening in the capital. Take time to reflect on your wine experiences, explore the city at your own pace, or relax and prepare for the mountain adventures ahead. This free time allows you to savor the memories of Kakheti's exceptional wines and hospitality.

  • Return journey to Tbilisi capital;
  • Free evening for personal exploration;
  • Time to reflect on wine experiences;
  • Explore city at your own pace;
  • Optional shopping or dining;
  • Prepare for mountain adventures;
  • Check-in and overnight in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: Free evening
  • Driving duration: 2 hours

Day 5: Tbilisi – Jvari – Mtskheta – Ananuri – Kazbegi

[+]

Journey to the stunning landscapes of Kazbegi, visiting the ancient capital Mtskheta with its UNESCO World Heritage sites including Jvari Monastery. Stop at the magnificent Ananuri fortress complex and continue through mountain passes to reach the breathtaking Caucasus highland region of Kazbegi with views of Mount Kazbek.

  • Visit UNESCO site Jvari Monastery;
  • Explore ancient capital Mtskheta;
  • Discover significant religious landmarks;
  • Stop at magnificent Ananuri fortress;
  • Journey through Caucasus mountain passes;
  • Arrive in breathtaking Kazbegi region;
  • Check-in and overnight in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 3.5 hours

Day 6: Kazbegi

[+]

Immerse yourself in the stunning landscapes of Kazbegi with a full day of mountain exploration. Visit the iconic Gergeti Trinity Church perched dramatically against Mount Kazbek's backdrop. Experience the raw beauty of the Caucasus Mountains and soak in the magnificent highland scenery that makes this region one of Georgia's most spectacular destinations.

  • Full day exploring stunning Kazbegi;
  • Visit iconic Gergeti Trinity Church;
  • Experience dramatic Mount Kazbek views;
  • Immerse in Caucasus mountain beauty;
  • Photography in magnificent highland scenery;
  • Explore raw mountain landscapes;
  • Overnight stay in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours

Day 7: Kazbegi – Stalin Museum in Gori – Uplistsikhe – Tbilisi

[+]

Travel from the mountains to explore Georgia's fascinating historical layers. Visit the Stalin Museum in Gori for insights into Soviet history, then walk through the ancient rock-hewn streets of Uplistsikhe, dating back to the early Iron Age. This day showcases the diverse historical periods that have shaped Georgian culture and heritage.

  • Depart from magnificent Kazbegi region;
  • Visit Stalin Museum in historic Gori;
  • Learn about Soviet era history;
  • Walk through ancient Uplistsikhe caves;
  • Discover early Iron Age heritage;
  • Experience diverse historical periods;
  • Return to Tbilisi and overnight.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 4 hours total

Day 8: Tbilisi – Gelati – Motsameta – Kutaisi

[+]

Journey to western Georgia to explore UNESCO-protected heritage sites. Visit the magnificent Gelati Monastery, a UNESCO World Heritage site showcasing medieval Georgian architecture, and the serene Motsameta Monastery. These spiritual centers represent centuries of Georgian Orthodox tradition and architectural achievement in beautiful natural settings.

  • Journey to western Georgia region;
  • Visit UNESCO-protected Gelati Monastery;
  • Admire magnificent medieval architecture;
  • Explore serene Motsameta Monastery;
  • Discover Georgian Orthodox traditions;
  • Experience architectural achievements;
  • Check-in and overnight in Kutaisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 4.5 hours

Day 9: Kutaisi – Prometheus Cave – Martvili Canyon – Kutaisi

[+]

Be mesmerized by the surreal formations of Prometheus Cave with its spectacular underground chambers and geological wonders. Continue to the scenic Martvili Canyon for boat rides through picturesque blue waters surrounded by dramatic canyon walls. These natural attractions showcase Georgia's diverse geological treasures and stunning landscapes.

  • Explore mesmerizing Prometheus Cave;
  • Discover surreal underground formations;
  • Experience spectacular geological wonders;
  • Visit scenic Martvili Canyon;
  • Enjoy boat rides through blue waters;
  • Marvel at dramatic canyon landscapes;
  • Return to Kutaisi for overnight.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours

Day 10: Kutaisi – Mestia

[+]

Journey to the remote mountain region of Svaneti, where timeless villages and ancient traditions await. Travel to Mestia, a highland town featuring traditional Svan towers and serving as gateway to one of Europe's most unique mountain cultures. This scenic journey takes you into Georgia's most preserved highland region.

  • Journey to remote Svaneti mountain region;
  • Travel to highland town Mestia;
  • Discover timeless villages and traditions;
  • See traditional Svan towers;
  • Enter unique mountain culture region;
  • Experience Georgia's preserved highlands;
  • Check-in and overnight in Mestia.
  • Duration of the tour: 6-7 hours
  • Driving duration: 5 hours

Day 11: Mestia – Ushguli – Mestia

[+]

Explore the timeless villages of Mestia and visit Ushguli, one of the highest inhabited villages in Europe. Experience the distinctive Svan culture, visit the Svaneti Museum, and marvel at the ancient towers that define this remarkable highland landscape. This day offers deep immersion into Georgia's most unique mountain heritage.

  • Explore timeless village of Mestia;
  • Visit Ushguli, Europe's highest village;
  • Experience distinctive Svan culture;
  • Visit informative Svaneti Museum;
  • Marvel at ancient defensive towers;
  • Deep immersion in mountain heritage;
  • Return to Mestia for overnight.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours round trip

Day 12: Mestia – Batumi Botanical Garden – Batumi

[+]

Travel from the highlands to the serene Black Sea coast and vibrant coastal city of Batumi. Visit the world-renowned Batumi Botanical Garden featuring diverse plant collections in spectacular coastal settings. This dramatic journey from mountains to sea showcases Georgia's incredible geographic diversity and natural beauty.

  • Travel from highlands to Black Sea coast;
  • Journey to vibrant coastal Batumi;
  • Visit world-renowned Botanical Garden;
  • Explore diverse plant collections;
  • Experience spectacular coastal settings;
  • Discover incredible geographic diversity;
  • Check-in and overnight in Batumi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 5 hours

Day 13: Batumi – Lively Squares and Black Sea Coast Relaxation

[+]

End your adventure in Batumi exploring the lively squares including Europe Square and experiencing the famous Ali and Nino statue. Enjoy relaxation along the serene Black Sea coast, providing the perfect setting for reflection on your comprehensive Georgian journey through ancient history, breathtaking landscapes, and rich cultural traditions.

  • Explore lively Batumi squares;
  • Visit elegant Europe Square;
  • See famous Ali and Nino statue;

  • Perfect setting for journey reflection;

Day 14: Batumi – Tbilisi (Train)

[+]

Conclude your adventure with a picturesque train ride back to Tbilisi, soaking in the last glimpses of Georgia's diverse landscapes. The comfortable train journey offers beautiful views as you travel from the coastal region through diverse terrain back to the capital, providing time to reflect on your extraordinary 15-day Georgian experience.

  • Picturesque train ride to capital;
  • Soak in last glimpses of landscapes;

Conclude your ultimate 15-day Georgian adventure with final moments in Tbilisi. Enjoy a leisurely breakfast and take time for any last-minute shopping or exploration. Transfer to Tbilisi International Airport for your departure, carrying with you unforgettable memories of Georgia's cultural heritage, world-class wines, natural and architectural wonders, and the complete exploration of this captivating land where ancient history, breathtaking landscapes, and rich traditions come together.
