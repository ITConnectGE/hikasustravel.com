Tour Overview

Dive into the wonders of Georgia on this 8-day adventure, blending cultural discovery, historic landmarks, and exhilarating outdoor activities. Begin your journey in the lively streets of Tbilisi, uncover the secrets of ancient monasteries, and savor the flavors of Georgia's renowned wine regions. Experience the natural beauty of Lagodekhi National Park with scenic hikes, and feel the rush of rafting on the Pshavi Aragvi River. Marvel at the iconic Gergeti Trinity Church, perched against the stunning backdrop of the Caucasus Mountains. This tour is perfect for travelers seeking a mix of culture, history, and active pursuits, offering a dynamic way to experience Georgia's diverse landscapes and traditions.

Tour Itinerary [+]

Day 1: Arrival and City Tour in Tbilisi

[+]

Begin your 8-day cultural and adventure journey with arrival in Tbilisi, Georgia's vibrant capital. Explore the historic Old Town with its narrow cobblestone streets, visit the ancient Narikala Fortress overlooking the city, and discover the unique architectural blend of traditional and modern landmarks. Experience the captivating atmosphere of this Caucasus gem that beautifully merges ancient history with contemporary life.

  • Arrival in Tbilisi and hotel check-in;
  • Explore Tbilisi Old Town;
  • Visit Narikala Fortress;
  • Walk through historic districts;
  • Discover local culture and cuisine;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 4-5 hours
  • Driving duration: 30 minutes

Day 2: Tbilisi – Bodbe – Sighnaghi – Lagodekhi

[+]

Journey to the Kakheti region, known for its wine heritage and spiritual sites. Visit the peaceful Bodbe Monastery, connected to Saint Nino and Georgia's Christian history. Continue to Sighnaghi, the charming "City of Love" with cobblestone streets and breathtaking views of the Alazani Valley. End your day in Lagodekhi, gateway to the stunning national park that awaits tomorrow's adventure.

  • Depart Tbilisi for Kakheti region;
  • Visit Bodbe Monastery;
  • Explore Sighnaghi, the "City of Love";
  • Enjoy views of Alazani Valley;
  • Arrive in Lagodekhi;
  • Overnight in local cottage or guesthouse.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours

Day 3: Lagodekhi – Hiking at Lagodekhi National Park – Telavi

[+]

Immerse yourself in the pristine natural beauty of Lagodekhi National Park. Experience scenic hiking trails leading to spectacular waterfalls, breathe in the fresh mountain air, and discover the park's diverse flora and fauna in this untouched wilderness. After your adventure in nature, continue to Telavi, the heart of Georgia's wine region, surrounded by rolling vineyards.

  • Explore Lagodekhi National Park;
  • Scenic hiking trails to waterfalls;
  • Experience pristine natural environment;
  • Wildlife observation opportunities;
  • Travel to Telavi wine region;
  • Check-in and overnight in Telavi.
  • Duration of the tour: 7-8 hours
  • Hiking duration: 4-5 hours
  • Driving duration: 1.5 hours

Day 4: Telavi – Vardisubani – Cooking Master-Classes – Tbilisi

[+]

Dive deep into Georgian culinary traditions today with hands-on cooking master-classes. Visit Vardisubani village and learn to prepare authentic Georgian dishes using traditional methods and local ingredients. Master the art of making khachapuri, khinkali, and other beloved Georgian specialties. This immersive cultural experience offers insight into Georgian family traditions and hospitality before returning to Tbilisi.

  • Visit Vardisubani village;
  • Participate in cooking master-classes;
  • Learn traditional Georgian cuisine;
  • Prepare khachapuri and khinkali;
  • Experience local hospitality;
  • Return to Tbilisi;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Cooking classes duration: 4-5 hours
  • Driving duration: 3 hours

Day 5: Tbilisi – Jvari – Mtskheta – Uplistsikhe – Dinner at the Winery – Tbilisi

[+]

Explore Georgia's rich historical and spiritual heritage today. Visit the UNESCO World Heritage sites of Jvari Monastery and Svetitskhoveli Cathedral in Mtskheta, Georgia's ancient capital and religious heart. Continue to Uplistsikhe, the remarkable ancient rock-hewn town showcasing early Georgian civilization. Conclude your day with dinner and wine tasting at a local winery, celebrating Georgia's ancient winemaking traditions.

  • Visit Jvari Monastery (UNESCO World Heritage Site);
  • Explore Mtskheta and Svetitskhoveli Cathedral;
  • Discover Uplistsikhe ancient cave town;
  • Learn about Georgian archaeological heritage;
  • Dinner and wine tasting at winery;
  • Return to Tbilisi;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 9-10 hours
  • Driving duration: 3.5 hours

Day 6: Tbilisi – Rafting – Ananuri – Kazbegi

[+]

Experience the thrill of adventure today with rafting on the Pshavi Aragvi River, feeling the rush of white-water rapids surrounded by stunning mountain scenery. After your rafting adventure, visit the picturesque Ananuri Fortress, a stunning architectural complex overlooking the Aragvi River. Continue to Kazbegi, nestled in the dramatic Caucasus Mountains, for breathtaking alpine landscapes.

  • Depart Tbilisi for adventure activities;
  • Rafting on Pshavi Aragvi River;
  • Experience white-water rapids;
  • Visit Ananuri Architectural Complex;
  • Scenic drive through Caucasus Mountains;
  • Arrive in Kazbegi;
  • Overnight stay in Kazbegi.
  • Duration of the tour: 8-9 hours
  • Rafting duration: 3-4 hours
  • Driving duration: 4 hours

Day 7: Kazbegi – Gergeti Trinity Church – Stalin Museum in Gori – Tbilisi

[+]

Marvel at one of Georgia's most iconic landmarks today. Visit the breathtaking Gergeti Trinity Church, perched dramatically against the stunning backdrop of Mount Kazbek and the surrounding Caucasus Mountains. This spiritual site offers unparalleled panoramic views and photographic opportunities. Continue to Gori to explore the Stalin Museum, learning about this controversial historical figure before returning to Tbilisi.

  • Visit iconic Gergeti Trinity Church;
  • Enjoy panoramic views of Mount Kazbek;
  • Experience Caucasus mountain landscapes;
  • Travel to Gori;
  • Explore Stalin Museum;
  • Learn about Georgian Soviet history;
  • Return to Tbilisi;
  • Overnight stay in Tbilisi.
  • Duration of the tour: 9-10 hours
  • Driving duration: 5.5 hours

Day 8: Departure from Tbilisi

[+]

Enjoy your final morning in Tbilisi, reflecting on your incredible 8-day cultural and adventure journey through Georgia. Take some last moments to explore the city, perhaps pick up souvenirs or enjoy a final Georgian breakfast. Check out from your hotel and transfer to Tbilisi International Airport for your departure, carrying unforgettable memories of Georgia's diverse landscapes, rich culture, adventure activities, and warm hospitality.

  • Final morning in Tbilisi;
  • Free time for last-minute exploration;
  • Hotel check-out;
  • Transfer to Tbilisi International Airport;
  • Departure from Georgia;
  • End of memorable 8-day adventure.
  • Duration: As per flight schedule
